The Poverty We Don’t See

Campaign Goal
Raise awareness about hidden poverty and food insecurity while building trust in transparent donation systems.

Which one is poverty?

Three men appear troubled, but the viewer cannot easily identify who represents poverty. The uncertainty challenges assumptions about what poverty looks like.

What does poverty really look like?

Poverty often exists quietly behind normal lives. The cave symbolizes isolation and hidden struggle.

"Need food to survive."

A human story behind statistics. The crying ID card represents hidden hardship behind everyday identities.

Where does your money go?

Many people hesitate to donate because they cannot see the impact of their contributions. This visual highlights the need for transparency.

Transparency Builds Trust

Showing real results helps build credibility and motivates people to participate in social impact campaigns.
